About me

My name is Paula Guagliardi. I am a designer and a cyanotype artist.

The cyanotype process is an alternative photographic process that produces a Prussian blue print.

No kind of ink is used to obtain the image, but it is the sunlight that, when coming into contact with the sensitized paper, forms the image due to the chemical reaction.

I use real plants to make my prints. I collect them by walking around and looking at what is already there and is only discovered by being present. Lately I have been working with other elements, like threads, exploring the technique deeper.

Blau is my homage to the cyanotype process and Anna Atkins. It's about nature and complex but yet simple things.
