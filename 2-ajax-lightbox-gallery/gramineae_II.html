Gramineae II

This original cyanotype was made from a real plant I collected previously. This is an original print which means there is only one available.

The cyanotype is a handmade process that involves coating the paper with a light-sensitive emulsion, exposing the print to sunlight or UV light, and then rinsed and dried. Each print is unique and results in different blues and tones variations.

Cyanotype's details:

  • Width: 17 cm ; Height: 24 cm (6.7x9.2 in.)
  • 300 gr. acid-free watercolor paper.
  • The cyanotype is signed and dated.
